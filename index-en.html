Our Mission

GCSI is a registered non-profit organization in Canada, founded to support immigrants, refugees, and vulnerable communities in Canada and Africa. Our mission is to provide vital assistance, promote resilience, and foster inclusion through multisectoral programs including sports, education, health, and livelihoods.
